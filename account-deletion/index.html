Request Account Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your First Capital Financial mobile app account and associated personal data.

You can also delete the signed-in account inside the app from Account Settings by typing DELETE and choosing Delete Account.

Request By Email

Email support@firstcapitalfinancial.xyz with your request.

Include

  • The email address used for your app account.
  • Your full name.
  • A clear statement that you want to delete your First Capital Financial mobile app account.
  • A safe way for us to contact you if identity verification is needed.

Do Not Include

Do not include passwords, authenticator codes, full account numbers, government ID numbers, or other sensitive documents in the initial email.

What Happens Next

We may need to verify your identity before completing the request. After verification, we will delete or de-identify app account data that is no longer needed, subject to legal, regulatory, security, fraud prevention, audit, dispute resolution, and legitimate business recordkeeping requirements.

Some financial, compliance, transaction, audit, or legally required records may need to be retained even after your app account is deleted.
