Privacy Policy for First Capital Financial Mobile App

Effective date: May 13, 2026

First Capital Financial respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the First Capital Financial mobile app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and authentication details.
  • Client profile information displayed in the app, such as contact details, meeting information, employment information, financial profile fields, mortgage/property information, and investment-related profile fields.
  • Account relationship information, such as related-account access requests and approvals.
  • Security information, such as multi-factor authentication status, session information, and device-login notification records.
  • Support and account deletion request information that you submit to us.

How We Use Information

  • Provide secure access to client profile and account information.
  • Verify identity and protect accounts with email verification and multi-factor authentication.
  • Support related-account access workflows.
  • Send operational notifications, such as account-link review notifications or security notices.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and account deletion requests.
  • Maintain security, compliance, audit, and business records.

Service Providers

We use trusted service providers to operate the app, including Supabase for authentication, database, and backend functions; Resend for operational email delivery; and Expo/EAS for mobile app build and release infrastructure.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs in the current app.

Security

The app uses HTTPS, Supabase Auth, email verification, multi-factor authentication, Row Level Security, and secure native session storage. No method of transmission or storage is perfect, but we use reasonable safeguards designed to protect personal information.

Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app, satisfy business and legal requirements, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain compliance records. Some financial, audit, security, or legal records may need to be retained after an account deletion request.

Account Deletion And Privacy Requests

You can delete your app account from inside the app under Account Settings, or request deletion help from our public account deletion page: https://firstcapitalfinancial.xyz/account-deletion.

We may need to verify your identity before completing a request. Some information may be retained where required or permitted by law, regulation, compliance obligations, fraud prevention, security, or legitimate business recordkeeping.
